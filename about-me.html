My name is HOANG ANH KY (Vitural Hwang).
Visual Artist / Producer / Director

A visual artist who is fascinated by all the “invisible & visible” visual elements that exist in and out of this universe, as an awe-inspiring by that hence the name “Vitural”.

I've been a multimedia freelancer for three years and specializing in visual development for one year.
I studied and practiced in the subconsciousness field, which gave me a significant advantage in exploiting the essential fundamental element of the "concept" and efficiency of the work. I also developing general skills in entrepreneurship, writing, cinematography, strategic & branding,
and psychology.
